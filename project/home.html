About Me

Hi There!!! I'm Velo and I'm a custom character artist. I do comissions for anyone and everyone looking for character art, twitch art and other things along those lines. - I'm a D&D enthusiast, as well as a gamer which is where a lot of my examples come from. Most is art from my campaigns, or fanart from games. You can see more examples and people I've collabed with on instagram @Velossoraptor. - I love to draw, and love helping other's bring their characters to life! (or at least to paper and print haha)
